# Hey! I am Kouyakou-Abalo SIMSOBA 👋

I am a **Google DeepMind Scholar** currently pursuing an **M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence for Science** at the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS) in South Africa. My mission is to bridge the gap between complex mathematical theory and transformative AI solutions.

My research expertise lies at the intersection of **Deep Learning** and **Mathematical Statistics**. I am particularly driven by the challenge of making AI models more resilient to the "noise" and outliers inherent in real-world scientific data.

## Research Interests
<u></u>

* **Robust Machine Learning:** Developing loss functions and architectures that handle imperfect data.
* **Time Series Forecasting:** Leveraging LSTM and deep learning for epidemiological and socio-economic predictions.
* **AI for Science:** Applying NLP, Large Language Models (LLMs), and Computer Vision to accelerate scientific discovery.
* **Explainable AI (XAI):** Ensuring that complex models remain transparent and interpretable for researchers.

## Beyond the Lab: Teaching & Impact
<u></u>

Before joining AIMS, I spent several years sharing my passion for science as a **Mathematics and Physics teacher** in Togo. I delivered high school-level instruction in:
* **Mathematics:** Probability, Set Theory, Spatial Geometry, Polynomials, and Complex Numbers.
* **Physics:** Kinematics, Wave Phenomena, Electrostatics, RLC Circuits, and Thermodynamics.

I also served as a **Volunteer Tutor** at the École Polytechnique de Lomé, instructing Master’s students in **Inferential Statistics** (Hypothesis Testing, Parameter Estimation) and **Optimization** (KKT Conditions, Gradient-Based Methods).
