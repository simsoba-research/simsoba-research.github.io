# Hello, I'm Kouyakou-Abalo SIMSOBA 👋

I am a **Google DeepMind Scholar** currently pursuing an **M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence for Science** at the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS), South Africa. My academic journey bridges mathematical theory, statistical modeling, and real-world AI applications — with a strong emphasis on impactful, interpretable, and ethical AI research.

---

## 🧠 Research Interests

- **Robust Machine Learning**  
  Designing resilient architectures and loss functions to handle imperfect or noisy data.

- **Time Series Forecasting**  
  Using LSTM and other deep learning methods to model and predict complex temporal dynamics in epidemiological and socio-economic contexts.

- **Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI)**  
  Enhancing the interpretability of deep models to promote transparency in high-stakes domains.
